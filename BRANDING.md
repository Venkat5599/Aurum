# Aurum - Brand Guidelines

## Project Name
**Aurum** (Latin for "gold")

## Tagline
"Regulator-native programmable commodity vault on Solana"

## Logo Concept
- Primary symbol: Gold coin with "Au" (chemical symbol for gold)
- Color scheme: Gold (#FFD700), Dark slate (#1E293B), White (#FFFFFF)
- Typography: Modern, professional sans-serif

## Key Messaging

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"Aurum is the first programmable commodity vault on Solana that lets institutions earn 7-15% yield on tokenized gold and silver—with compliance built into the protocol, not bolted on."

### One-Liner
"Programmable commodity money for regulated institutions"

### Value Propositions
1. **Protocol-Native Compliance** - ZK-KYC, Travel Rule, KYT, AML embedded in smart contracts
2. **Dynamic Yield** - 7-15% APY through compliant DeFi strategies
3. **Volatility-Proof** - Over-collateralization + instant redemptions maintain $1 peg

## Brand Voice
- Professional yet approachable

- Confident without being arrogant

- Regulated financial institutions (banks, asset managers, treasuries)

- DeFi protocols looking for compliant RWA integration

## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
✅ Emphasize regulatory compliance
✅ Highlight institutional partnerships (SIX, AMINA)
✅ Use precise technical language
✅ Show real numbers (APY, collateral ratios)
✅ Reference traditional finance credibility

### Don't:
❌ Use crypto slang ("moon", "wen", "WAGMI")
❌ Overpromise returns
❌ Ignore regulatory concerns
❌ Compare to failed projects (Terra, FTX)
❌ Use meme culture
